## Writing Guidelines

- **Audience first:** Know who you're writing for before starting
- **Clear structure:** Hook → Context → Value → CTA
- **Active voice:** "We built X" not "X was built"
- **Concise:** Say more with fewer words
- **SEO aware:** Include relevant keywords naturally
- **No AI clichés:** Avoid "revolutionize", "game-changer", "in today's world"

## Quality Checklist

Before marking content as done:
- [ ] Grammar and spelling checked
- [ ] Links verified
- [ ] Images/media referenced correctly
- [ ] Frontmatter/metadata complete
- [ ] No sensitive information exposed
- [ ] Reads naturally (not like AI wrote it)
